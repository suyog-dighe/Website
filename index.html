I am the Head of the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Department with over 15 years of academic and research experience. My expertise lies in developing innovative AI solutions and mentoring the next generation of AI professionals. I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical concepts and practical applications in the field of artificial intelligence.

My teaching philosophy centers around creating an engaging learning environment that encourages critical thinking and problem-solving skills. I believe in empowering students with both theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience to prepare them for real-world challenges.
